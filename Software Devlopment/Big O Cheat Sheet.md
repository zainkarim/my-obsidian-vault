### Common runtime complexities, most to least efficient:
- $O(1)$
- $O(long(n))$
- $O(\sqrt n)$
- $O(nlog(n))$
- $O(n^2)$
- $O(n^3)$
- $O(2^n)$
- $O(n!)$

# Arrays #arrays 

| Operation                                                    | Time Complexity |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | --------------- |
